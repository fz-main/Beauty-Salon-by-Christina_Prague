Premium Services

Bestseller ✦ 01 — PERMANENT MAKEUP

Powder Brows & Nano Architecture

The Powder Brow technique creates a soft, diffused, makeup-like finish using thousands of precise micro-pixels implanted beneath the skin. Combined with our exclusive Nano-stroke architecture for natural hair simulation, this treatment delivers brows that are perfectly shaped, symmetrical, and tailored to your unique facial structure. Lasts 2–3 years with a single touch-up.

From 4,500 CZK · 90–120 min
◈ 02 — LASH STUDIO

Lash & Brow Architecture

Lash lamination, lift, and tint treatments that redefine the eye contour without the daily ritual. Our Brow Lamination service restructures and sets brow hairs in a lifted position for up to 8 weeks of effortless grooming. Combined with professional tinting, it creates a polished, structured frame for the eyes.

From 1,800 CZK · 60 min
❋ 03 — SKIN AESTHETICS

Advanced Glow Protocols

Our signature Glow Skin treatments combine micro-needling, hyaluronic infusion, and LED phototherapy to restore luminosity and youthful density to tired skin. Each protocol is individually prescribed following a detailed skin analysis. Suitable for all skin types and tones.

From 2,900 CZK · 75 min
⬡ 04 — MICROBLADING

Hair Stroke Precision

Classic microblading using a hand-tool blade to deposit pigment in ultra-fine, hair-like strokes beneath the skin. Ideal for sparse brows requiring natural definition. Results appear painterly and genuine — indistinguishable from natural brow hair when executed with expert precision.

From 3,800 CZK · 100 min
◉ 05 — LIP STUDIO

Lip Blush & Contour

A semi-permanent cosmetic tattoo technique that enhances the natural colour and shape of the lips. Lip Blush adds a gradient flush of pigment from the border inward, creating the appearance of fuller, more symmetrical lips with a beautifully defined Cupid's bow. Heals to a soft, stained-lip effect lasting 2–4 years.

From 4,200 CZK · 110 min

How It Works

The Christina Method

01
Consultation

An in-depth face mapping and pigment analysis session to identify the perfect shape, colour, and treatment plan tailored to your unique facial structure.

02
Design

Using digital symmetry tools, we draft the precise brow, lip, or eye design before any pigment is applied. You approve every millimetre before we begin.

03
Application

Executed with calm precision using only premium, dermatologically tested European pigments. Topical anaesthetic ensures maximum comfort throughout.

04
Aftercare

A detailed aftercare protocol and aftercare kit is provided. A complimentary follow-up touch-up is included within 6 weeks of your initial visit.
